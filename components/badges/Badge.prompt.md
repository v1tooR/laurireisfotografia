Small uppercase pill — tags a service category, a session type, or acts as an eyebrow above a serif title.

```jsx
<Badge variant="gold">15 Anos</Badge>
<Badge variant="gold-outline">Newborn</Badge>
<Badge variant="soft">Estúdio</Badge>
```

Variants: `gold` (filled, default), `gold-outline` (hairline, for over photos/dark), `navy`, `rose`, `slate` (soft accents), `soft` (tinted gold on light). Keep label text short — 1–3 words.
