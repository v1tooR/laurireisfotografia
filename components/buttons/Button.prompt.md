Uppercase, wide-tracked action button — use for any primary or secondary call to action across Lauri Reis surfaces.

```jsx
<Button variant="gold" size="lg" onClick={book}>Agendar ensaio</Button>
<Button variant="outline-light" as="a" href="#portfolio">Ver portfólio</Button>
```

Variants: `gold` (filled, default), `outline` (gold hairline on light), `outline-light` (white hairline on photos/dark), `dark` (navy fill), `ghost` (subtle on light). Sizes `sm | md | lg`. On dark navy or over photos use `gold` or `outline-light`; on paper use `gold`, `outline`, or `ghost`.
