Underline-style labelled input for contact and booking forms. Gold focus line. Pass `textarea` for the message field.

```jsx
<Field label="Nome" placeholder="Seu nome completo" required />
<Field label="Tipo de ensaio" placeholder="Ex.: 15 anos, newborn…" />
<Field label="Mensagem" textarea placeholder="Conte sobre o momento que deseja registrar" />
```

Designed for light (paper) surfaces. Stack in a grid with `gap: 24px`.
